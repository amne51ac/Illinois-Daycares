About Illinois Daycares

Illinois Daycares is an independent map of licensed child care providers published from public Illinois Department of Children & Family Services (DCFS) licensing data. It is designed to help families explore providers by location, facility type, and other fields available in the source listings.

Data sources

  • Provider listings originate from the state’s public tools, including the DCFS Sunshine provider lookup. A helper script in this repository can export results by county; those exports are normalized into a CSV that feeds the geocoding build.
  • Street-level coordinates are resolved with the U.S. Census Bureau Geocoder where possible. Addresses that do not match are placed near the center of the ZIP code with a small deterministic offset so nearby points remain distinguishable.

Map layers

Base maps may include © OpenStreetMap, © CARTO, Esri, and OpenTopoMap contributors (see on-map attributions). “Satellite + streets” overlays imagery with roads and labels for context.
