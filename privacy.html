Privacy policy

IllinoisDaycares.com is a static website. This page describes what happens in your browser and what third parties may see when you use the site. It is meant as plain-language guidance, not legal advice.

What this site does not collect

There are no accounts, logins, or forms that send your personal information to a custom backend run by this project. The public JSON dataset is the same for everyone; the site does not know who you are.

Data stored on your device

The map may save favorites, saved search names, and related UI preferences in your browser using localStorage. That data stays on your device unless you clear site data or use another browser. It is not uploaded to a server operated by this project as part of normal use.

Share links

When you copy a “share link,” the URL can encode your current filters, search text, and—if you placed a reference pin—approximate coordinates and a label. Anyone who receives the link can see those values. Do not share links if you want to keep that information private.

Hosting and third parties

The site is typically served via GitHub Pages, which will handle requests for HTML, JSON, and assets like any static host (standard access logs may apply per GitHub’s policies).

The pages load fonts from Google Fonts, map libraries (for example from unpkg/jsDelivr), and map tiles from the providers shown in the map attribution. Those services may process technical data such as your IP address when your browser requests their resources. See their respective privacy policies if you need detail.

Children

This site is a family-planning aid for adults. It is not directed at collecting information from children.

Changes

This policy may be updated occasionally. The site footer links here for the current version. Effective as of March 2026.
